### More SDK support
Ensure you have `org.freedesktop.Platform.Compat.i386` `org.freedesktop.Platform.ffmpeg_full.i386`
`org.freedesktop.Sdk.Compat.i386` `org.freedesktop.Sdk.Extension.toolchain-i386` installed.
Run the flatpak with the `android-studio` flatpak/bin script to include the SDK on your $PATH
